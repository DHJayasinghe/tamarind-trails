What is Tamarind Trails?

Sri Lanka is best
experienced slowly.

At Tamarind Trails, we believe Sri Lanka is best experienced slowly — with time to taste, listen and connect.

We design small-scale, sustainable journeys for UK travellers who want more than a checklist of sights. From misty hill country and ancient cities to quiet coastal villages and jungle hideouts, our itineraries are crafted by Sri Lankans who know the stories behind the scenery.

Every journey respects our communities, wildlife and environment — so your holiday brings joy not just to you, but to the island too.

  • Locally designed & operated from Sri Lanka
  • Small-scale, sustainable & thoughtfully paced
  • Crafted for UK travellers who care

Our Impact

People, Planet & Paws

When you travel with Tamarind Trails, your journey does more than move you from A to B.

Planet

Travelling Lighter

Slow, logical routes with less backtracking. Scenic train journeys. Partners who minimise plastic and respect wildlife habitats. Travel that treads gently on the island we call home.

People

Supporting Local Lives

Locally owned guesthouses, fairly paid guides and drivers, community cooking classes and craft workshops. Your money supports real Sri Lankan families, not big anonymous chains.

Paws

Caring for Street Dogs

For every Tamarind Trails booking, we donate £15 to street dog welfare in Sri Lanka. Your holiday helps give Sri Lanka's street animals a safer, kinder future.
